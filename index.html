Know when to seek immediate care

Contact a veterinarian or emergency animal hospital if your pet shows severe breathing difficulty, loss of consciousness, uncontrolled bleeding, repeated seizures, or suspected poisoning.

This website provides general educational information and does not replace professional veterinary advice.
